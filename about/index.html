About

I am Li-Yung Chen, a software engineer based in Berlin, Germany. My work spans frontend (React, TypeScript), backend (Node.js, Java), and DevOps on AWS—from warehouse management systems at Amazon to robotics integration pipelines and large-scale document processing side projects.

I hold an M.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Technical University of Munich (Dipl.-Ing. TUM), with master’s thesis work on efficient sound-event classification using BNNs on power-constrained IoT devices at ETH Zürich.

Alongside tech, I teach German language and culture online to learners in Taiwan.
